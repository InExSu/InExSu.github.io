常见问题 / FAQ

Q: Why do I see “No duplicates found”?
A: The add-on ignores empty cells. Make sure your selection contains duplicate non-empty values.

Q: How to use the add-on?
A: Select any range → Add-ons → Ephemeris → “🔍 Find duplicates in selection”.

Q: Does it work with multiple ranges?
A: Yes! Hold Ctrl (Cmd on Mac) and select multiple ranges — duplicates will be found across all of them.
